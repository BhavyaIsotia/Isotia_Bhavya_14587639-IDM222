Hi, I’m Bhavya

I’m a fourth-year undergraduate at Drexel University in the Pennoni Honors College, majoring in Computer Science with a minor in User Experience & Interaction Design. I recently wrapped up a project management internship at Exelon and I’m excited to keep learning across user research, product design, psychology, and front-end development.

I design with empathy and code with curiosity. My approach blends human-centered research, clear interaction patterns, and visual craft to make digital experiences that are useful, accessible, and delightful.

When I’m not working on digital or artistic projects, you’ll probably find me dancing, exploring new restaurants, showing up for student org events, traveling, or trying something completely new just to learn from it.
